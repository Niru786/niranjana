Travel in
Sirmaur

Sirmaur district is the southernmost district of Himachal Pradesh,
northern India. It is largely mountainous and rural, with 90%
of its population living in villages.Some of its towns include the
capital Nahan, Tuheri, Bhawan, Shamra, UchaTikker and Suketi,
the latter known for Shivalik Fossil Park.
